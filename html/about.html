About us



Who are we?

We are a small business based in Cape Town but ship nation-wide.
We make custom jewellery, as well as sell a collection of classic bracelets which are also hand made.
Our goal is to make sure every customer receives a product that suits their needs and wants.



Our story

During the harsh Covid-19 lockdown, I found myself crafting jewellery to keep me busy.
To my surprise, a lot of people loved the bracelets I made and wanted me to hand make bracelets for them, and even their friends and family.
From there on i began a small business from home, making and selling charm bracelets. My business became quite popular
and so I decided to expand and create a website whereby customers can order online and have their order shipped anywhere in South Africa!



Custom accessories

Our custom accessories range from bracelets, anklets, necklaces and even keychains!
To create a custom order, please use the contact form in the Contact us section and specify "custom" in your message.
I will then contact you to create your custom Accessorie(s).
